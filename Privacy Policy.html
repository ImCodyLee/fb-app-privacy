Privacy Policy — MMM Repost App

Last updated: [2025-07-10]

Summary

MMM Repost App connects your Facebook / Instagram account to post or read media and basic profile information so that your Instagram posts can be shared to a Discord channel. We only request the minimum permissions required to perform that task.

Data we collect

  • public_profile — your name and public profile ID as provided by Facebook/Instagram.
  • pages_show_list, instagram_basic — to access the Instagram Business/Creator account and read media (images, captions, permalinks).
  • We store access tokens for the account you authorize (securely) so the app can fetch new media and post it; tokens are refreshed/rotated as permitted by Facebook.

How we use the data

We use these values only to detect new Instagram posts and publish them to a Discord webhook/channel you configure. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties except for services you explicitly connect (e.g., Discord). We may use hosting/CDN providers to store and deliver content.

Data retention & deletion

We store access tokens and the minimum metadata required to avoid posting duplicates. To request deletion of your data or to disconnect your account, contact us at contact@macmillermemoir.com.

Security

We follow reasonable security practices to protect stored data (encrypted storage for tokens, HTTPS for all endpoints). However, no internet service is perfectly secure.

Contact

If you have questions, contact: contact@macmillermemoir.com
